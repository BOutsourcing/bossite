Careers

Technology is an ever evolving tool used to achieve business goals, and the hardware and software of today stands as the pinnacles of humanity and are essential for development of modern business.

At Business Outsourcing Services, we are responsible for the role technology plays in achieving our clients' goals. We are the makers and believers who apply the latest technology to build innovative businesses on their behalf. As an employer, we foster friendly company culture that respects our employees' integrity, freedom of thought and commitment. We love what we do and the challenges that arise every day as we do it.

Our teams are agile, proactive and professional, actively sharing their knowledge and successes. We stay on top of emerging technologies, helping each other live up to his or her full potential and professional development. The work is challenging but the rewards are never lacking, we value the effort, knowledge and professionalism that each employee brings to our team. Above all, we nurture and respect transparency, honesty, team spirit and dedication to work.

Work

Essential to all of our personal success is a balance between work and private life. While we push ourselves to build the best technology, we do so because we like to. As a company, we support this, whether it's mentoring one another, attending conferences, or hosting meetups.

Life

But we also like other things, each of us our own. Some of us are more out-going, other more family-oriented. It's why we are so well-organized and agile at work. We respect one another's private lives and always support and celebrate each other's personal success.

Atmosphere & Environment

Our office is located at the top floor of New Belgrade's Blue Center building, one of the city's finest work spaces. Light fills the open space where you can find us mingling in the kitchen, discussing the latest conference or technology release; hard at work, coding; or grouped in a team, resolving a difficult problem together. During breaks we hang around on a wraparound balcony that reveals one of the most beautiful views of Belgrade you can find.

We share knowledge, work on challenging projects, visit conferences, organize team events, connect and grow together. This also means that we're actively involved in quick integration and professional development of our new colleagues. Our programmers are the best in their field and we respect them for what they do and the value they bring to the company, both as individuals and professionals, by offering competitive salaries, private health insurance, education, travels, various activities, and above all, a pleasant working environment.
