Projects

Business Outsourcing Services is present in the area of software development business since 2010. We have been working on a variety of projects as a long-term partner and our portfolio counts many different projects. Our team has successfully provided and run software and web development solutions and services for companies of all sizes and with various needs such as designing server architecture, server maintenance, building new apps and new platforms, website migration projects, developing content management systems, web template design and many other. Some of the latest projects we have been working on are listed here.

AD oppfølging - Employee monitoring tool

www.adgruppen.no

GIPS / Oppfølging is a most used HR application in Norway which provides a unique web-based management tool for a more comprehensive monitoring of the employees. The solution ensures that all the employees are taken care of in the organization and that all employee information are available at any time or place. Evolution Software is modern and user-friendly management tool with many convenient features for efficient and better follow-up of staff.

APPSCO - applications management tool

www.appsco.com

Appsco is an innovative and cloud-based application for personal and business use. Appsco project is dedicated to developing a perfect access management solution. The general idea behind this project is a simple, time saving and secure management of different applications and users without sharing login credentials and easy access with a single sign-on.

Mysiteshop - Website creation tool

www.mysiteshop.com

MySiteShop is a website market with pre-made website templates. MySiteShop is an easy-to-use website builder, with clean and responsive design, suitable for any business. Regardless of their skill level, users can build a fully-functional website. Project is based on a simplified editor and variety of features where users can easily add content and publish their website in just a few steps.

Mypro - Football players database

mypro.jpy.fi

MyPro is web platform designed to help sport unions in tracking their members around the world. It improves communication between union and their members, gives access to complete players up-to-date statistics, manages member subscriptions and eases free agents trading. MyPro is exclusively used by Union of Finnish top football players, which is also part of an international player association FIFPro.
